BIO

With a name inspired by the yellow teletubby “Lala” there wasn’t much hope that Laneikka would grow up to be anything but a creative. Laneikka is a Sydney based actor, writer and artist working in film, fashion and photography. At the heart of all her work is an immense passion to give a voice to the voiceless, by telling stories that aren’t being told. The admiration she has for women in her own life and their own exploration of sexuality, humour and identity is something she continues to grapple with in her work.

Recent highlights:

In 2021, Laneikka will tour the show “Follow Me Home” with Australian Theatre For Young People nationally around Australia.

Her debut full-length play DEADSKIN. was awarded the State Theatre Company x Flinders University Young Playwrights Award, with a world premiere as part of KXT 2021 season, she will also be acting in the work.

She was a recipient of Q Theatre’s 2020 Q Lab grant to undergo developing a new work titled SHITHOLE.

Her photographic series "Femmes Fructueuses" (Fruitful Woman) was shortlisted by Aesthetica Magazine.

She will be travelling to Tokyo, Japan in 2021 as a selected participant in the International Theatre For Young People’s 21st World Congress to develop works with other international theatre-makers.

Her styling work has been featured in international online fashion magazines including Sticks and Stones Agency, Vogue Italia, Elle AU, Period Magazine, BEAU NU Magazine, Elegance Magazine etc.
